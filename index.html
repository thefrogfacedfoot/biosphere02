~/about.md

hi, welcome in 🌿

this is my corner of the internet — a quiet biosphere sealed in glass, half forest and half star map. drag the windows. plant a seed. draw your own constellation.

nothing here is locked. nothing tracks you. it's just a place.

  • built without frameworks (just html / css / vanilla js)
  • everything is draggable, resizable, minimizable
  • your state survives a refresh (localStorage)
~/devlog/

devlog #03 — jun 26

added the constellation studio: click sky to drop a star, click two stars to draw a line, double-click to name your shape. shapes persist. now the night sky belongs to whoever visits.

also tuned the firefly drift — was too uniform, felt mechanical. switched to perlin-ish wandering and they finally feel alive.

devlog #02 — jun 24

windows now snap to edges, remember position, and bring-to-front correctly. titlebar drag uses pointer events so trackpads / touch work. minimize sends them to the taskbar instead of vanishing.

spent way too long picking the green for the moss text. settled on #8fd49a against deep-space navy. feels like phosphorescence.

devlog #01 — jun 22

first paint. star canvas, gradient nebula, three placeholder windows. the guide had a flat desktop background; i wanted the page itself to feel like a habitat, so the background got two parallax layers and a slow aurora wash.
